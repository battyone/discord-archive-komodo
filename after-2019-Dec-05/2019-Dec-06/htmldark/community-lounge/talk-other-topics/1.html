The difference between the dollar and the gold from the Bitcoin is Bitcoin increases participants in global supply chain and blockchain solves problems of trust in supply chain. The dollar or gold has no ability to execute contracts beyond the state-controlled financial system. If the government to which the producer belongs extremely controls foreign exchange, the producer will receive the currency in its own currency, which is worthless. The manufacturer then loses the incentive to keep the promise, so the buyer does not initially place an order for the troubled producer. However, if bitcoin is paid that does not go through the financial network, the producer will be tempted to deliver it on time without considering being taken over by the government, so hundreds of millions of new global citizens can participate in the global supply chain. Blockchain boosts confidence among the numerous stakeholders involved in the supply chain. Most wines imported to nation are subject to the hot equator, which makes them taste bad. The use of refrigerated containers does not guarantee proper temperature. However, if temperature sensors inside a container record abnormalities in blockchain, not only retailers but also end consumers can compare information and prices. Due to blockchain, value-added product lines will be added to global supply chain and more opportunities will be opened.
