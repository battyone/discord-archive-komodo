Discussion area for GameCredits, a peer to peer cryptocurrency for the gaming industry. The first non-Komodo chain to adopt dPoW. This purpose-built cryptocurrency aims to launch a store where users can buy and sell their PC games using GameCredits. Additionally, the currency aims to be accepted across major vendors such as Steam, Draft Kings, Fan Duel, Green Man Gaming, etc to realize the ultimate vision of GAME which is a p2p currency for gamers without lock in on any specific platform.
