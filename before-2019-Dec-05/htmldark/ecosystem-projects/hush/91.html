Hush - Speak and Transact Freely Private Cryptocurrency and Messenger on Zero Knowledge Proof Encryption. Hush is an implementation of the Zerocash protocol, offering a high standard of privacy preserving confidentiality of transaction metadata. Hush codebase is based on Komodo and Zcash 2.0.x. Hush is one of the first blockchains to enforce only Sapling transactions from the beginning. No other blockchain has started as a pure Sapling chain.
