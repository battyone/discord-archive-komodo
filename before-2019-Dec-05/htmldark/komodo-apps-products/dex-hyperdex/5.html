lukechilds 23-Jun-18 09:18 AM
@webworker01 thanks for the detailed feedback, all of those issues appear to be issues with marketmaker though, not HyperDEX, we literally just send/display data to/from marketmaker
>sometimes the buy/sell buttons get greyed out or blink and don't seem to do anything and the app needs a restart We grey out the button until marketmaker responds to the buy API request. Due it's blocking single threaded nature sometimes requests can take a very long time >sometimes the bobs don't show up anymore and the app needs a restart We poll marketmaker for the order book once per second and display it's output. If orders aren't showing up it's because marketmaker isn't telling us about them >sometimes it seems that the bids/asks displayed either aren't really there or aren't responding to being matched properly Same as above really. >almost always need to enter an offer higher than what is displayed but when the swap is completed you get close to the price displayed We just pass the values directly to marketmaker, all order matching logic is in marketmaker. >*not sure how utxos are handled but as i get down to the bottom of what i sent in to buy with the trades aren't close to my amount i wish to buy anymore where they were closer in the beginning with a single utxo, i need to now make multiple offers to get to my target buy amount We don't handle UTXOs at all, it's entirely handled by marketmaker
Unfortunately we can't fix any of these things in HyperDEX, it needs to be fixed in marketmaker. There is a v2 re-write in the works that will hopefully resolve these issues
