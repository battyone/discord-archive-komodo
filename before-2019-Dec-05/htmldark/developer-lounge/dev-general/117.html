Mihailo 05-Feb-19 08:25 AM
@Alright @dukeleto Channels CC is a payment mechanism like LN but on-chain. There is no coordination needed between Alice and Bob. Think of channels CC as a gift card for some store. You top up the card with funds (open a channel to Bob which will add funds to that channel on a 1of2 address), and then you use the card for paying (make a payment to Bob - releasing funds from channels address to Bob). And it is instant, no need to wait for confirmations/notarizations for a payment, as when opening channel with funds that tx needs to be notarized and from that point Bob is sure that he will get the funds as soon payment is made (gets in mempool). Mihailo 05-Feb-19 01:58 PM
@dukeleto No. Alice needs to open a channel (create a channelopen tx) and that is it. The channel term is used to describe the behaviour of CC, but basically Alice sends funds to CC 1of2 address and that is the channel. In order for funds to be spent from that address, the creator (Alice) supplies password (hash) when issuing payment. This prevents Bob from spending it like it would be on regular 1of2 address without CC. (edited)
