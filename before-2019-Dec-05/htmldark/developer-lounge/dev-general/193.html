CMaurice 09-Jul-19 03:56 PM
I made a mother of all docs EPUB for my own consumption by 'find'ing and 'cat'ing and 'pandoc':
find docs/ -name '*.md' ! -name '*README*' -exec cat {} \; > moad.md
or
find komodo-api/ -name '*.md' ! -name '*README*' -exec cat {} \; > komodo-api.md
for just the api docs, but don't do those without editing out the massive arse hex chunks in rawtransactions.md and blockchain.md first: I backtraced a segfault to libpcre choking on them. then
pandoc moad.md --toc -s -o moad.epub
should get you an EPUB with toc for conversion and uploading with Calibre This EPUB has no title, cover image, any prettiness and a very large toc, but it's good for me to skim and traverse while waiting for old ladies to do their shopping. I'll learn more about pandoc and prettifying over the next weeks, but questions: when might be a timeframe to aim for when the docs will settle a bit? Is this useful to anyone else?
