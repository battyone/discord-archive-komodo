DigitalWealthCreator you must move those funds from that seed to a new seed created using the Verus agama or else you might lose them 1) Download https://github.com/VerusCoin/Agama/releases/tag/v0.6.0.13-PBaaS-Cross-chain-Technology-Preview 2) First, create a new wallet (new seed) 3) Note down the address 4) Logout then Login at least two times to verify that the seed is giving you the same address everytime 5) Then import the old seed (compromised), in the Verus Agama wallet and activate KMD lite mode.
