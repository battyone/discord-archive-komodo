@DesignBlock Independent blockchains (aka "assetchains") are fully independent in essentially the same manner as any two different chains are. In the super-shortest explanation: The only "link" that happens is with dPoW, and that's where a notary creates a transaction on the chain and that transaction carries data (which is interpreted and used in the same manner as conventional "checkpoints") (edited)
