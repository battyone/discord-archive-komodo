make a set . of moving averages of longer and longer periods
when the price is above the fastest MA, that MA above the next slower MA, .... all the way to the slowest, SELL
when the price is below the fastest MA, that MA is below the next slower MA, ... all the way to the slowest, BUY
this will end up only buying when extremely oversold on all time periods and selling when overbought on all time periods
depending on the MA periods you choose it might only issue a few trades, but the rarer the trades, the higher chance they will be profitable
