This arb bot will bring in all the liquidity we need, this then brings in users then in brings people just setting limit orders directly without the bot Now there will be a % at which some think is too small to make a profit from but others will still take it. The market will self balance with people leaving and people coming. So now we have solved the liquidity issue. Now CEX will want to hook into OUR liquidity as we will have a GLOBAL orderbook. We will become the center of the crypto space, interconnecting all the exchanges and all the coin communities
There will be some game theory in play. Just like BTC miners, when the diff goes up, some stop mining as they are making a loss, once prices go up they start mining again. The liquidity bot will be the same. At 5% every man and their dog will be doing it but with competition it might go all the way to 1.5% above CEX, then most people think it is not worth it. But then since the liquidity is lower people are willing to pay 2% or 3% then people get back in. So the market will have a balance of liquidity providers and the lowest % profit they will take to provide that service.
