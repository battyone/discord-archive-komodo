coqui
A grassroots cryptocurrency originally launched in June 2017 as an educational tool, the goal of which was to help people and businesses in Puerto Rico learn how to use blockchain. Since it's creation Coqui Cash has evolved into much more capable of being used as a currency and as a public data registry. This channel is best suited to students and developers in Puerto Rico who want to learn about Blockchain and is seeking a partnership on a government level.
