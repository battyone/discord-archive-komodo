@chinmaydshah notary centralisation is touched on in the whitepaper. for the hypothetical case that notaries became centralised, the worst they could do is not notarise to btc. for this to happen, more than 50 out of the 64 notary nodes need to conspire with each other to have that effect.
the voting process is open to all kmd holders. notary elections are open to anyone. approximately 30 of the 60 available (out of 64) notary nodes are elected each year. the other 30 are automatically elected. what decides auto-re-election? the top performing notary nodes (the ones that participate in the multisig transaction to btc, "the notarisation"), so it is in the notaries interest to perform their function to be auto-re-elected, otherwise, they must stand for election with freshly enthusiastic candidates.
