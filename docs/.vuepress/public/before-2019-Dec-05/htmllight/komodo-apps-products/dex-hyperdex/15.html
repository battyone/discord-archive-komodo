lukechilds 08-Aug-18 09:33 AM
@Cryptoon I don't think CMC rate limiting will stop your order books showing up. I often get rate limited by CMC and my order book still shows. Sometimes it does take a while for them to sync. Can you view the requests in the network tab and verify they are getting through to market maker? You could also try running _api.debug({method: 'getpeers'}) in the console to see how many peers you are connected too. If you're poorly connected that may be why
But sometimes and on certain networks it does just take a very long time for order books to show, this is something I've noticed myself and is a problem with the underlying marketmaker daemon. We poll it once a second for order book data and as soon as it gives us some info, we show it.
