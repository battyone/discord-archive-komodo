ComputerGenie 23-Jul-18 02:42 PM
The biggest current issue with GPU mining VRSC is PCIe bandwidth. Depending on setup, the hashrate of a 1080Ti ranges anywhere from 2-145 MH/s. GPU slotted direct to 16x in board is best (and even an unpowered 16x riser will drop rate some) and 1x-16x riser slotted in 1x gen2 port is the worst.
board, slot, power, CPU, and w/out riser(s) all play a big role in what a card does with Verus
best results I had on my z170-pro was with only 1 1080Ti and it was direct in slot
because of how the board is laned, just the act of having 2 cards on that board drops card0 by 31% (from 145 to 101) (edited)
