why do you need the 24 words passphrase if you have your compressed WIF already ? If that's for using the genkomodo.php then here's the tweak: - replace $k in $bitcoinECDSA->setPrivateKey($k) (line 141) with your private key in hex format https://github.com/DeckerSU/komodo_scripts/blob/master/genkomodo.php#L141 - ofc you'll need to convert your compressed WIF to the private key, here's how: convert your WIF from base58 to hex, using a tool like this (offline of course) http://lenschulwitz.com/base58 once you obtain your WIF in hex format , for example :
BC10A4928997C1A45BE602806F3DA292206F66EB4BFD36FBE431EB0DBFE4CC414C01168A5381
remove the first 2 characters (BC = 188 in hex which is the wif type of KMD) & the last 10 characters (01-this is the compression byte and 168A5381 - this is the first 4 bytes of the double SHA256 checksum of all the previous bytes). Then you'll have:
10A4928997C1A45BE602806F3DA292206F66EB4BFD36FBE431EB0DBFE4CC414C
This is the private key that you'll need to insert in the first step
