be careful giving out txid. combined with access to your wallet, some of the tx details are extractable
if you sent it, a lot of details could still be in your wallet via the opid, though doing a z_getoperationresult seems to make a lot of it go away, not sure if it is totally purged though.
even as a recipient giving out the txid allows someone with access to the wallet to see the amount and the memo field
however, who sent it is not visible. what zaddr received is not there either, though it would likely be possible to correlate that with the wallet
it is quite impressive how little data is available even with full access to the wallet

1

so if you have a bunch of ztx and you want to reduce the data exposed, do a z_getoperationresult. but keep in mind that after you do that, even with a blockchain rescan, all you would be able to extract are the txid/amount/memo
