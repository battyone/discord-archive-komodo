@wolfgang what type of competition is it going to be? Also are the participants developer heavy or not really? This is to give me an idea of what is the best fit for you. Generally Antara is the simplest route and the best looking as you can get the wallet, explorers, etc with Antara. If your community is heavy developers they may be more interested in the nitty gritty parts of it, and you may want to explore the documentation for command line interface.
