I see it totally differently. As stated during elections notaries job is to "notarize" : secure kmd and ecosystem by running programs that devs (and mostly jl777) release. Linking other activities like ecosystem development is not relevant, at least to me : your job as a notary is to protect the network and be very reactive. Developping side business is not a part of the deal : it helps the entire ecosystem but relation with notaries duty is in my mind a : 0. What is related is everything and every process that can help refine adapt and make the notarizing system better, period : for example you have 2 huge contributors : @webworker01 that developped a wonderful interface to monitor every notary activity / performance : THIS is a way to optimize the notarization system because it shows publicly strenghts and weakness of any operator and it just hardens the competition to be on top stats : top stats job well done.
