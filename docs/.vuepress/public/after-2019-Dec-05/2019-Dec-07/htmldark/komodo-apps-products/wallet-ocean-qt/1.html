erav3n 06-Dec-19 08:05 PM
Here is small example how to export address and amount fields to CSV for 10 transactions:
komodo-cli listtransactions "*" 10 | jq -r '[.[] | {address, amount}] | (map(keys) | add | unique) as $cols | map(. as $row | $cols | map($row[.])) as $rows | $cols, $rows[] | @csv'
Result will be like:
"address","amount" "RF7ahEsFqdskMe6jS9TzeEk6npRjqtjnCw",-0.01209162 "RF7ahEsFqdskMe6jS9TzeEk6npRjqtjnCw",0.01 "RF7ahEsFqdskMe6jS9TzeEk6npRjqtjnCw",0.01 "RF7ahEsFqdskMe6jS9TzeEk6npRjqtjnCw",0.01 "RF7ahEsFqdskMe6jS9TzeEk6npRjqtjnCw",12.012906 "RF7ahEsFqdskMe6jS9TzeEk6npRjqtjnCw",0.01 "RF7ahEsFqdskMe6jS9TzeEk6npRjqtjnCw",0.01 "RF7ahEsFqdskMe6jS9TzeEk6npRjqtjnCw",0.01 "RF7ahEsFqdskMe6jS9TzeEk6npRjqtjnCw",0.01 "RF7ahEsFqdskMe6jS9TzeEk6npRjqtjnCw",0.01
